# Competitor Research — Findings From Analysis

Research findings from competitor and inspiration site analysis. Referenced when positioning new builds.

---

## Personal Brand / Investor Sites (April 2026)

### Sahil Bloom (sahilbloom.com) — BEST REFERENCE for dark investor brand
- Dark/black background, white text, monochromatic
- Numbered sections (01, 02, 03) — creates institutional authority
- Portfolio company logos prove real deal activity
- 800K+ newsletter subscribers, NYT Bestselling book
- Clean separation: content arm vs investment arm

### Codie Sanchez (codiesanchez.com) — BEST for personality-driven dark brand
- Dark navy/charcoal backgrounds — premium feel
- NYT Bestseller badge prominent
- Media logos: Forbes, CNN, Bloomberg, WSJ
- "Movement" framing, not "I'll teach you"
- Casual language signals authenticity

### Alex Hormozi (acquisition.com) — BEST for conversion structure
- $250M+ portfolio revenue, specific exit numbers
- Action-oriented CTAs ("I'M READY TO SCALE")
- Multiple conversion paths (workshop, courses, quiz)
- Feels like a platform, not a personal page

### Greg Isenberg (gregisenberg.com) — Hyper-specific numbers
- 158,485 subscribers (not "150K+") — real data signal
- Text-focused, minimal design — ideas are the product
- TikTok and Reddit advisory roles = blue-chip credibility

### Naval Ravikant (nav.al) — End-state minimalism
- Zero self-promotion, reputation precedes
- Ultra-minimal — NOT a template for someone building a brand
- Only works with massive name recognition

### Nick Huber (sweatystartup.com) — Stats grid + named companies
- 7 companies, 325 employees, 820K followers — stats grid at top
- Named portfolio companies with logos
- Employee count is an unusual and powerful metric

### Key Pattern: Newsletter as primary CTA
- Almost every top personal brand site leads with newsletter capture
- Funnel: free content → email list → book/course → paid community → high-ticket

### Australian Gap
- Almost no modern, tech-forward investor personal brand sites in AU market
- Wide-open positioning opportunity

## Bitumen/Asphalt Competitors (April 2026)

### Key competitors to BitumenCo
- Ace Asphalt (Gold Coast)
- Asphalt Concepts (Brisbane/GC/SC)
- Hot Driveways QLD (since 2002, 20+ years)
- Australian Asphalt (50 years, GC)
- Custom Asphalt (30+ years, North Brisbane/SC)
- Colossal Asphalt (family-owned, Brisbane/GC)

### Industry-specific content that works
1. Before/after transformations (cracked → fresh is visually dramatic)
2. Aerial/drone shots of completed work
3. "Bitumen vs Asphalt vs Concrete" comparison content
4. Driveway calculator (length × width × price/m²)
5. Aftercare/warranty terms (differentiator)
