# Design System — Industry-Specific Patterns

Colour, font, and layout choices by industry/project type. Reference before starting any new build.

---

## By Industry

### Construction / Trades (Bitumen, Concrete, Roofing)
- **Theme:** Dark backgrounds (black/charcoal)
- **Palette:** Monochrome base. Accent only if brand has one.
- **Fonts:** Space Grotesk (headings) + Inter (body). Industrial, technical feel.
- **Layout:** Hero → How It Works → Services (split resi/commercial) → Pricing → Gallery → About → Service Areas → CTA
- **Key sections:** Before/after gallery, pricing transparency, process explainer
- **Notes:** These are considered purchases. Lead with process and pricing, not urgency.

### Pest Control
- **Theme:** Light or dark — depends on brand
- **Palette:** Greens, teals (clean/safe signals) or dark with trust colours
- **Fonts:** Clean sans-serif. Nothing playful — pests are serious.
- **Layout:** Hero with urgency → Trust signals → Services → Areas → Testimonials → CTA
- **Key sections:** Urgency signals, safety credentials, service area map
- **Notes:** Urgency-driven purchase. Safety concerns early. "Same day service" messaging.

### Electrical
- **Theme:** Dark works well (wiring, industrial)
- **Palette:** Blues, whites (electrical/trust signals)
- **Fonts:** Clean, professional sans-serif
- **Layout:** Hero → Trust bar (licences, insurance) → Services → About team → Areas → CTA
- **Key sections:** Licence numbers, insurance proof, team photos (working in people's homes = trust)
- **Notes:** Trust is the #1 factor. People let electricians into their home. Show faces, credentials.

### Personal Brand / Investor / Strategist
- **Theme:** Dark (premium/exclusive)
- **Palette:** Black/charcoal base, white text, one muted accent max
- **Fonts:** Geist or Space Grotesk (tech-forward). No serif fonts.
- **Layout:** Hero (bold statement) → What I Do → Who I Work With → Proof (big numbers) → Current Focus → CTA
- **Key sections:** Large stat numbers, filter section (who it's NOT for), minimal everything
- **Notes:** The restraint IS the credibility signal. Less is more. No stock photos. No long paragraphs.

### Carpentry / Handyman
- **Theme:** Warm, natural tones
- **Palette:** Wood tones, warm grays, greens
- **Fonts:** Friendly but professional sans-serif
- **Layout:** Hero → Services → Gallery (project photos) → About → Areas → CTA
- **Key sections:** Gallery is king — show the work. QBCC licence in footer.

---

## Universal Rules

1. **Never use stock photos.** Source from client's site, Instagram, Google listing, or skip.
2. **Always include ABN in footer** for Australian businesses.
3. **Mobile-first.** Every layout decision starts with mobile.
4. **4 fields max** on any lead capture form.
5. **Homepage form is mandatory.** Never make contact a separate page only.
6. **Match the brand's actual colours.** Don't impose your preferences.
