# Wins — What Worked Well

Validated patterns to repeat in future builds. Updated after every project.

---

## Design

### 1. Dark theme for dark-product industries
**Project:** BitumenCo
**Why it works:** Asphalt is black. Bitumen is black. A dark website feels natural for the product. Don't fight the industry's visual language.
**When to apply:** Any industry where the product/material is dark (asphalt, roofing, automotive).

### 2. Space Grotesk + Inter font pairing
**Project:** BitumenCo
**Why it works:** Space Grotesk (headings) has a technical, industrial feel. Inter (body) is clean and readable. Good contrast between display and body text.
**When to apply:** Construction, industrial, tech-adjacent trades.

### 3. Gallery with hover-reveal info
**Project:** BitumenCo
**Why it works:** Shows project photos clean, reveals category + title on hover without cluttering the visual. Modern UX pattern.
**When to apply:** Any portfolio/gallery section across all projects.

## Content

### 4. Using only real images from client's existing site
**Project:** BitumenCo
**Why it works:** Every image is their actual work — honest, verifiable. No stock photos to undermine trust.
**Rule:** Always source images from client's existing site, Instagram, or Google listing. Never use stock.

### 5. Pricing transparency on homepage
**Project:** BitumenCo
**Why it works:** Putting price ranges front and center builds trust and pre-qualifies leads. Visitors who see pricing and still enquire are serious.
**When to apply:** Any trade/service where pricing is a top-3 question (driveways, pest control, electrical).

### 6. Real owner story in About section
**Project:** BitumenCo
**Why it works:** Used Jack's actual narrative — builder who discovered bitumen, created a family business. Genuine and compelling. People connect with real stories.
**Rule:** Always use the real story. If the owner's story isn't available, skip the section rather than fabricate.

### 7. ABN in footer for Australian businesses
**Project:** BitumenCo
**Why it works:** ABN is a verifiable trust signal. Shows legitimacy. Takes 5 seconds to add.
**Rule:** Always include ABN for Australian business websites.

## Technical

### 8. Comprehensive SEO metadata on every page
**Project:** BitumenCo
**Why it works:** Custom title, description per page + targeted keywords. Strong SEO foundation from day one.
**Rule:** Every page gets custom metadata. Don't leave defaults.

### 9. Navbar services dropdown with anchor links
**Project:** BitumenCo
**Why it works:** Quick access to specific services without cluttering main nav. Efficient navigation pattern.
**When to apply:** Any site with 4+ services.

## Research & Competitor Analysis

### 10. Dark theme + numbered sections for premium personal brands
**Project:** Wolf Partners
**Why it works:** Sahil Bloom, Codie Sanchez patterns — dark background + numbered sections (01, 02, 03) creates institutional authority. Feels premium and exclusive.
**When to apply:** Personal brand / investor / strategist sites.

### 11. Specific numbers over vague claims
**Project:** Wolf Partners (research)
**Why it works:** "$80M+" hits harder than "extensive experience". Greg Isenberg shows 158,485 subscribers, not "150K+". Specificity signals real data.
**Rule:** Always use specific numbers. If the client has real metrics, use exact figures.

### 12. Brand gold used sparingly as accent
**Project:** Wolf Partners
**Why it works:** Wolf Partners logo uses gold (#C5A44E). Using it only for section labels, the dot in the headline, and bullet numbering keeps it premium without overwhelming. Matches their actual brand.
**When to apply:** When a brand has a strong accent colour, use it as seasoning not the main dish.
